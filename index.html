V and Jennie fan art

Requested Art by Ellen, c.

77 cm × 53 cm (30 in × 21 in)

In a grand royal palace, BTS’s V and BLACKPINK’s Jennie stand side by side, exuding modern royalty. V is dressed in an elegant, royal-inspired outfit that blends traditional Korean hanbok elements with high fashion, while Jennie wears a flowing pastel gown adorned with delicate embroidery and a radiant tiara. The palace's towering marble columns and intricate golden details surround them, as warm golden light streams through massive windows, illuminating lush gardens and ornate fountains beyond. Their calm yet powerful expressions radiate elegance and influence, creating a striking scene where timeless royalty meets contemporary stardom.


Hollow Purple

Shining light of a Blackhole

700 cm (280 in) × 880 cm (350 in)

Gojo Satoru's "Hollow Purple" is a mesmerizing and destructive technique that embodies his immense power and mastery of space manipulation. When he activates it, a swirling sphere of energy forms in his hand, a perfect fusion of his "Cursed Technique: Reversal Red" and "Cursed Technique: Blue." The sphere is an eerie combination of deep violet and dark blue hues, with streaks of black and white lightning-like energy crackling around it. As Gojo releases the attack, the sphere rapidly expands, distorting the space around it. The air trembles and warps, as the Hollow Purple erases everything in its path, leaving behind an empty void. The attack doesn't just destroy — it obliterates the very existence of anything caught within its radius, as though the targets were never there. The contrast of the swirling dark energy with Gojo's serene, confident expression adds an even more intense and chilling atmosphere to the spectacle. The sheer force of the technique reflects Gojo's untouchable status, a combination of elegance, danger, and overwhelming power.
