As Pain stepped off the small aircraft in Seattle, he was immediately struck by an eerie silence. The bustling city he had anticipated was replaced by an unsettling stillness, the streets devoid of life. A thick fog hung in the air, casting an otherworldly glow over the dilapidated buildings. He looked around, confusion washing over him; something was distinctly wrong. The only sound was the soft rustle of the wind and the faintest hint of distant whispers, like ghostly echoes from another time. He realized he had landed in a universe far removed from the one he knew—a world where shadows lurked, and the sun seemed to be perpetually hidden behind ominous clouds.

As he ventured deeper into the city, it became clear he was not alone. Glimpses of figures flitted through the shadows, their movements graceful yet predatory. Panic surged through him as he recognized the unmistakable features of vampires—pale skin, elongated fangs, and predatory gazes that glinted with hunger. He quickly assessed his surroundings, heart pounding in his chest. There were no signs of human life, only remnants of what seemed to be an abandoned civilization. It dawned on Pain that he needed to adapt quickly if he wanted to survive this nightmarish reality.

Recalling stories of vampire lore, he knew the tales of their weaknesses—sunlight, garlic, and stakes through the heart. But in this world, knowledge alone would not guarantee his safety. He needed to navigate the perilous landscape with cunning and strategy. Moving cautiously, he sought shelter in a nearby building, its windows shattered and walls covered in ivy. Inside, he rummaged through the debris for anything that might aid him: a broken chair leg, an old kitchen knife, and a few garlic cloves from an abandoned kitchen. Each item was a potential lifeline, but he understood that sheer luck wouldn't keep him alive for long.

As night fell, the vampires would emerge, hunting for their next victim. Pain made his way to the roof, hoping for a vantage point to observe their movements. From his perch, he saw them gathering in the streets below, their inhuman elegance on full display. He understood that he had to make critical choices—strategic alliances with any surviving humans he might encounter, ambush the vampires when the odds were in his favor, or find a way to escape this cursed city altogether. The stakes were high, and every decision could mean the difference between life and death in this world ruled by darkness

A TWICE poster typically features the popular K-pop girl group TWICE, which consists of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Known for their catchy music, vibrant aesthetics, and synchronized choreography, the group has a massive international fanbase. Posters often showcase the members in thematic visuals related to their latest album or concept, incorporating bold colors, fashionable outfits, and dynamic poses.

The posters can vary in style depending on the era or concept, with some highlighting a cute, energetic vibe (like in their earlier "Cheer Up" and "TT" eras), while others may feature a more mature or chic look (like in their "Feel Special" or "Fancy" eras). These posters are highly collectible, often sought after by ONCE (the official fanbase of TWICE), and serve as a visual representation of the group's evolving style, artistry, and impact on the global music scene.
