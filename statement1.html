Artist Statement

Ben 10 follows the story of a young boy, Ben Tennyson, who stumbles upon a mysterious alien device called the Omnitrix during a summer road trip with his cousin Gwen and their Grandpa Max. The Omnitrix, which attaches itself to Ben’s wrist, allows him to transform into various alien species, each with unique abilities. Over the course of the original series, Ben learns to use the Omnitrix to fight villains and protect Earth from extraterrestrial threats, including evil aliens like Vilgax, a powerful warlord obsessed with obtaining the Omnitrix for himself. Alongside his family, Ben also uncovers surprising revelations, such as Grandpa Max’s secret past as a member of a covert organization called the Plumbers, who are dedicated to defending Earth from alien dangers. As Ben grows, so do the stakes, leading him to face increasingly dangerous foes while balancing the challenges of being a kid with the responsibilities of a hero.

As the Ben 10 series evolves across multiple iterations like Ben 10: Alien Force, Ultimate Alien, and Omniverse, Ben matures from a cocky, immature boy into a more responsible hero. The Omnitrix undergoes upgrades, allowing Ben to access more powerful alien forms, including Ultimate versions of existing ones. The series also explores deeper connections within the universe, such as the wider Plumber organization, the origins of the Omnitrix (created by the alien scientist Azmuth), and the various galactic powers and factions involved in Ben’s battles. Major recurring themes include the moral responsibility that comes with power, Ben's evolving relationship with his family and friends, and the idea that anyone can become a hero, even when faced with overwhelming odds. By the end of Omniverse, Ben has cemented his place as one of the most powerful heroes in the universe, capable of handling threats on a cosmic scale while still maintaining his humanity.
