Artist Statement

My name is Shadow, and I am currently a graduate student at San Jose University, where I study sociology. My ultimate goal is to create a stable life for myself, with a fulfilling career and a strong, loving family. While academics are a big part of my life, I am also deeply passionate about creativity and self-expression. Every month, I work on small projects, including love letters for my Pookie Bear, crafting thoughtful items, and exploring various artistic endeavors. This consistent practice helps me grow in my artistry and keeps my life balanced between academic growth and personal fulfillment.

One of the key goals I'm working toward is becoming a substitute teacher, as I love engaging with students and making a difference in their lives. Teaching allows me to work in a dynamic environment where I'm always learning and growing. Another dream I am pursuing is to become a police officer. This role would allow me to give back to my community in a tangible way, serving and protecting those around me. I’ve always gravitated toward positions where I can be active and hands-on, rather than confined to a desk job.

Teaching and policing both offer me that blend of activity and purpose. I enjoy environments where I can move, help, and make an impact rather than be limited to routine tasks. I thrive in situations where I’m constantly adapting, learning, and facing new challenges. This is why I have so much passion for both fields, and I aim to integrate them into my career in a meaningful way.
